    Om oss

    Who we are ?

    The council is a Moroccan-Norwegian body that was founded in order to promote the follwing goals :
    • Maintaining the links between the Norwegian Moroccans and their motherland
    • Strengthning the links that exist between the Norwegian people and the Moroccan one
    • Acting as a cultural bridge between Norway and Morocco
    • Raising the Moroccan government awareness of the challenges that face the Moroccan diaspora in Nothern Europe
    • Contributing to the development efforts undergoing in Morocco and defending our national cause

    How it all started ?

    Around midt-2015, Norwegian-Moroccans living in Oslo, Bergen and Stavanger started to think about founding a pan-Moroccan structure that can act as umbrella for all Moroccans in Norway and the Nordics.
    Many young people, women and men from diverse backgrounds and yrker joined in the discussion which culminated in the foundation of the Council in 2016.
    The Council is body that is not linked to any government, party or political body and is inclusive of all Moroccans regardless of sex, gender, region or religion.
